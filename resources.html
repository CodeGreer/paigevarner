Resources

This page lists books, websites, and Atlanta-area resources we have found useful. If you are looking for information on a topic not listed here, please feel free to contact us. We may be able to help you locate additional resources.


Pregnancy and Childbirth Preparation

Books:

The Birth Partner
Penny Simkin

Comprehensive information on pregnancy and labor geared toward the spouse or other birth partner. Also a good read during your pregnancy.


Birthing from Within: An Extra-Ordinary Guide to Childbirth Preparation
Pam England and Rob Horowitz

This empowering birth-preparation book treats birth as a rite of passage rather than a medical event. It encourages self-exploration and personal growth, and cultivates intuition.


The Pregnancy Book
William Sears and Martha Sears

Easy-to-follow, month-by-month information on what to expect during your pregnancy.

Breastfeeding

Books:

Breastfeeding Made Simple: Seven Natural Laws for Nursing Mothers
Nancy Mohrbacher and Kathleen Kendall-Tackett

Well-written instruction on getting started, that is great to read prior to birth. Includes strategies for common challenges.
